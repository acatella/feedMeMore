Board of Directors

View Ridge Board

We are a member-owned club, covered by our volunteer Board of Trustees. The Board meets monthly throughout the year, and is made of nine members who serve three-year terms. At the Club's annual meeting, held in March, members elect a slate of candidates, usually three per year, who can serve up to two three-year terms.

Questions, suggestions and comments from Members about the club and their club experience are always helpful for the Board, and are both welcome and encouraged. Please email us at suggestions@vrstc.org at any time. Our Member Communications Board Chair will respond back to you as soon as possible.
