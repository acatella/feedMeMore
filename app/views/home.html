NOTE TO THE MEMBERSHIP REGARDING BOARD NOMINATIONS

The VRSTC Board of Trustees is seeking individuals to fill at least 3 board seats for election at the Annual Meeting, which will be held on March 7, 2018. Trustees are generally elected for a term of three years with an expectation to prepare for and attend monthly board meetings as well as fulfill other duties as appropriate to the role. The Board is seeking to expand its competency in the arena of Technology as well as continued strength in roles concerning accounting/finance as well as strategic planning.

Members who may be interested in learning more about Board positions and responsibilities should email the Nominating Committee at vrstc@vrstc.org.

The Nominating Committee will be sharing its recommendations with the membership in late February.

We look forward to hearing from our membership!

About the Club

V iew Ridge Swim & Tennis Club was established in 1958 as a member-owned club where families could gather to enjoy swimming, friendships, and other activities. There are currently 480 member families. As a seasonal club, we are open annually from the middle of May until early September. The club offers organized programs including a Swim Team, Tennis Team, Water Polo Team, an Adult Swim Team, as well as Swim and Tennis lessons (both group and private instruction) during the operating season. Other activities include Early Morning Lap Swim, Open Swim, and up to three dedicated lap lanes available throughout the operating hours.
