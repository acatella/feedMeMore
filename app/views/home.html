About the Club

V iew Ridge Swim & Tennis Club was established in 1958 as a facility where families could gather to enjoy swimming, friendships, and other activities. Presently there are 480 families. We are open from the middle of May until the middle of September. We have a varied schedule offering a Swim Team, Tennis Team, Water Polo Team, and other activities such as Open Swim, Adult Lap Swimming, an Adult Swim Team, and many social activities.

Club Activities
